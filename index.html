History

Sagamore's founders have worked together since 2002 and have invested in over 100 funds and companies in Asia and the US. Sagamore's first investments focused on biotechnology and expanded to fund and direct investments in China with Sagamore China Partners I (SCP I) in 2006. The team raised subsequent funds SCP II (2010), SCP III (2013) and SCP IV (2016). In total, Sagamore has over $400m in assets under management for investing in opportunities in China.
