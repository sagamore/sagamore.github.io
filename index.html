History

Sagamore's founders have worked together since 2002 and have invested in over 100 funds and companies in Asia and the US. Sagamore's first investments focused on biotechnology and expanded to fund and direct investments in China with Sagamore China Partners (SCP) I in 2006. The team raised subquent funds SCP II and SCP III in 2010 and 2013. In total, Sagamore has over $250m in assets under management for investing in opportunities in China.
